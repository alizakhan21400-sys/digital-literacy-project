# Task 4: Email Etiquette and Social Media Checklist

In this task, I practiced professional electronic communication by drafting two formal emails: one requesting an assignment extension and another applying for an internship. This exercise helped me understand the importance of clear subject lines, formal salutations, and a polite tone. Additionally, I developed a social media checklist consisting of five "do’s" and five "don'ts" to maintain a clean and professional digital footprint. 

I learned that effective communication is not just about the message but also about the format and etiquette used. One challenge was ensuring the internship email was persuasive yet concise. I also reflected on common communication mistakes, such as using informal language or failing to proofread before sending. Mastering these skills is essential for my professional growth and helps in building a positive reputation in the academic and corporate world.
